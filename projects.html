Feu Follet

Çağan Korkmaz, Zihan Liu, Kettu Kautto, Shurong Han, Kathleen "Kai" Lindgren, Boris

A small sokoban-style puzzle game I worked on for a class with a group of 5 people. This project was my first experience with a group of this size, and a great experience in task management. I helped with general development in unity and later on with the art, we didn't have a artist on the team, so I had to learn the 3D art pipeline. I also went in more depth with the Unity animation system which was a good experience, since I find it really annoying to deal with.

I still use Blender for my personal projects and I quite enjoy doing so.

Animal Outage

This was a small jam game I made for the GN! Game Jam #17. You play as rat in a high voltage substation, you can climb the fences and walk around. If you touch any of the high voltage equipment you will die a very loud death. The game tries to convey the feeling of how close to dark magic electricty actually is.

I made it solo and decided to try out Godot for the first time. It was a nice learning experience and a good lesson in time management, where I spent too much time on modeling and aesthetics and not enough on the game. It was still cool learning about high voltage substations and modeling all the assets, Godot was excellent as well.
